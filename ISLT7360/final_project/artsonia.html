Artsonia is a great resource for our school! It is an online art museum just for kids! Every week I post the latest artwork for you to see and share with family! Setting up an account is super easy and you can share the account with friends or family member as well!

Another great thing about Artsonia is the gift shop! You can purchase different items with your child's artwork printed on it. A portion of the proceeds goes directly back to me to help with the purchase of art supplies! This is a great opportunity for me to introduce your child to as many art resources as possible! Without your support it wouldn't be possible!
