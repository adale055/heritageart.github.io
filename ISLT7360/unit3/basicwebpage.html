Mrs. Davis's Heritage Elementary Art!

Welcome to art at Heritage Elementary where we create all kinds of amazing things in our art room!
Each day our core values are embodied in our work in the art room. At School of the Osage, our core values are:

  1. Innovation
  2. Tradition
  3. Strength
  4. Opportunity
Everyday student's have the opportunity to successful and strong through innovation and tradition. The art room is a natural place for all of these values to come together

About Mrs. Davis

I grew up at Lake of the Ozarks and went to college at Drury University in Springfield. I studied architecture for 4 years before pursuing education. I have been an art teacher for 8 years at School of the Osage

I love watching my students grow creatively. Each of my students is their own artist.
