Welcome to art at Heritage Elementary!

We create all kinds of amazing things in our art room!
At School of the Osage, we emobody our core values in art every day!
Our core values are:

  1. Innovation
  2. Tradition
  3. Strength
  4. Opportunity
Students have the opportunity to successful and strong through innovation and tradition.
The art room is a natural place for all of these values to come together

About Mrs. Davis

I grew up at Lake of the Ozarks and went to college at Drury University in Springfield. I studied architecture for 4 years before pursuing education.
I have been an art teacher for 8 years at School of the Osage

I love watching my students grow creatively. Each of my students is their own artist.
